About me

HI, I'M PEARL.

A passionate animation enthusiast fueled by creativity and a love for storytelling. While I may not have professional experience just yet, my heart beats for the magical world of animation, where imagination takes flight. When I'm not sketching characters or bringing them to life on my computer screen, you'll find me lost in the enchanting realm of books, crafting stories of my own. With a curious mind and a knack for getting lost in the depths of creativity, I'm on a thrilling journey to unlock the wonders of animation. Join me as I explore new horizons and bring my imagination to life, one frame at a time.
